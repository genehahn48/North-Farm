Authored by Gene Hahn in March 2018

Interactive map showing field numbers for Plant and Soil Sciences Dept fields at North Farm in Lexington, KY. If hover above field boundary Principal Investigator (PI) name displays. If you click within field boundary PI name, email, phone #, and website.

Here is link to explain soil mapping units in more detail. This link will open PDF file that contains all soils surveyed within the North Farm boundary.


Data source and method

Created with QGIS 2.18.16 and Catro online with data sourced from the University of Kentucky Lee Moser, Steve Higgins, Sarah Wigthman, and Sara Carter. The soils mapping units sourced from Soil Survey Staff, Natural Resources Conservation Service, United States Department of Agriculture. Web Soil Survey. Available online at https://websoilsurvey.nrcs.usda.gov/. Accessed [03/25/2018]. https://websoilsurvey.nrcs.usda.gov/.
